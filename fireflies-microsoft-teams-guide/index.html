Fireflies for Microsoft Teams — Setup Guide

Quick setup

  1. Sign up with your work email (SSO recommended)
  2. Connect Microsoft Teams and your calendar
  3. Enable auto‑join for scheduled meetings
  4. Select a UK‑friendly summary template (Decisions / Next steps)
  5. Push notes to Teams channels & your task system

Automation ideas

  • After meeting → post summary to chosen Teams channel
  • Items tagged “Decision/Blocker” → create Planner tasks
  • Weekly digest → archive to SharePoint or OneDrive
